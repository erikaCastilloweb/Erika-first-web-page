Tower Bridge

Tower Bridgebuilt in 1894 over the river Thames, in the most famous bridge to see in London and its name comes from its proximity to the Tower of London, which is right next door.
On this stone drawbridge, you can enter to see the steam engine that lifts it working and climb to the top to walk on a transparent glass walkway while enjoying excellent views of the city.

Big Ben

In the Palace of Westminster you will find the British Parliament and Big Ben,its famous clock Tower, which over time has become one of the icons to visit in London.

Buckingham Palace

Buckingham Palaceis the official residence of Queen Elizabeth II and the place that has hosted many of the historical moments of London and the United Kingdom, as well as another of the places to visit in London.

Notting Hill

Notting Hill, famous for the Portobello Market and its beautiful colored houses, is our favorite neighborhood to visit in London.
